How this estimate works
We run your inputs through Loan Mountain Capital’s internal AVM trained on historical deals, using a Random Forest model and a business clamp (ARV between 1.0–2.0× total cost).

Max loan is then computed as:
Loan = 70% × ARV
Cash to close follows our standard MA-style structure (initial advance, fees, and setup costs).
